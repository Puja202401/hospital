Services

Health Care Solutions

Pathology Laboratory

A pathology laboratory provides diagnostic testing for diseases using bodily fluids and tissues.

Laparoscopy services

Laparoscopy services offer minimally invasive surgical procedures using a small camera and specialized instruments to diagnose and treat abdominal issues.

Gynecology services

Gynecology services focus on the female reproductive system, providing care for women's health issues, including menstrual disorders, contraception, and menopause management.

Pregnancy care

Pregnancy care services offer comprehensive prenatal care, including regular check-ups, screenings, and guidance for expecting mothers.

Antenatal checkups

Antenatal checkups involve regular medical appointments and screenings for pregnant women to monitor the health of both the mother and the developing baby.

IVF Consultant

An IVF consultant specializes in assisting couples with fertility issues through in vitro fertilization (IVF) procedures and related treatments.

Delivery and Postpartum Care

Delivery and postpartum care involve the process of childbirth and the care provided to mothers and newborns immediately after birth, including monitoring for complications and providing support for recovery.

Comprehensive Menopause Clinic

A comprehensive menopause clinic provides specialized care for women experiencing menopause, including symptom management and hormone therapy.

Premarital Counseling

Premarital counseling involves sessions with a therapist or counselor to help couples prepare for marriage by discussing various aspects of their relationship and future together.

PCOD Clinic

A PCOD clinic specializes in the diagnosis and management of Polycystic Ovary Syndrome (PCOS), offering treatments tailored to individual needs.

Personalized Care

Personalized care involves tailoring healthcare services to individual needs, preferences, and circumstances to ensure the best possible outcomes.
